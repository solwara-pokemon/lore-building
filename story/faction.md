# The Unforgotten Party

## Overview
The Unforgotten Party is the primary antagonist faction of Solwara. They are a **legitimate political party** with genuine public support, not a traditional evil team lurking in caves.

## Slogan
> **"The Land Remembers."**

A rallying cry that sounds noble — an act of defiance, a vow to never forget what was lost. By the end of the game the player understands the dark implication: the land does not remember. The land is gone. The people need to let it go.

## Nature
- Publicly: advocates for restoration research and reclamation efforts
- Privately: actively pursuing the awakening of Groudon
- They do genuine good — disaster relief, housing for survivors, infrastructure
- Their extremism is revealed gradually as the player peels back layers

## Their Position on Kyogre
The Unforgotten Party correctly identifies Kyogre as a victim — weaponized by Team Aqua, hated unfairly by Team Magma. Pokémon are not at fault for The War. This is one of the few things they have right.

They want to awaken Groudon not out of hatred for Kyogre but to rebalance the world. They frame it as freeing Groudon rather than weaponizing it — consciously trying to avoid repeating Team Aquas mistake.

Except they are repeating it anyway. Just with different justifications.

## The Tragic Flaw
**Groudon is dead.** The Unforgotten Partys entire plan is built on an impossibility. Nobody knows this — not even the party leadership. They are acting in complete good faith toward an unreachable goal.

This is not a story about institutional corruption or conspiracy. It is a story about **collective delusion** — grief pointed in an impossible direction by sincere people who never learned the truth.

## Visual Identity
- **Colors:** Terracotta, ochre, deep amber (dry land, soil, rock) with clean white accents
- **Aesthetic:** Clean, professional, almost corporate. Inspiring propaganda that looks genuinely hopeful.
- **Symbol:** A mountain peak or sunrise emerging from water — noble on the surface, with Groudons markings subtly worked into the negative space
- **Architecture:** Deliberately references pre-flood building styles

## Failure
When their plan collapses it should not feel like defeating Team Rocket. It should feel like watching someone finally have to accept a truth they have been running from for years.
