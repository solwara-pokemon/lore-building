# Player Roles

## Overview
The player chooses a role at the start of the game by selecting a starter. The role is not explained to the player — they simply pick a Pokémon. On replays, players recognize how their role shaped their entire experience.

Roles are displayed at starter selection with a short evocative description. The narrative meaning is left for the player to discover.

---

## The Hero
> *"Others look to you for answers."*

**Personality:** Brave, selfless, perhaps reckless. Carries the weight of others expectations. Inspires those around them without always knowing how.

**Arc:** The Hero spends the game being looked to for a solution. The "there is no fixing this" revelation hits them hardest — they were supposed to be the one who saved everything.

---

## The Doctor
> *"You believe every problem has a solution."*

**Personality:** Curious, driven, interventionist. Brilliant but sometimes arrogant. Always moving, always trying to fix things. Deeply empathetic but gets people hurt through conviction. Eccentric and unpredictable. Carries enormous loss.

**Arc:** Self-driven rather than externally driven. The Doctor runs toward danger believing they can solve it. Their acceptance arc is about learning that some things cannot be solved — only witnessed.

**Inspiration:** Deliberately evokes The Doctor from Doctor Who in player feel and emotional journey.

---

## The Accepted
> *"You have already made your peace."*

**Personality:** Calm, still, unbothered. Has made peace with the flooded world — but perhaps too easily. There is a difference between resignation and genuine acceptance, and this role begins at the wrong one.

**Arc:** The most nuanced arc. The Accepted already knows the answer but has to learn *why* it is the right answer. Their journey is discovering that their peace was actually avoidance.

---

## The Defector *(Unlockable)*
> Unlocked by completing the game with all three launch roles.

**Personality:** Conflicted. Formerly loyal to The Unforgotten Party. Carries guilt.

**Arc:** Begins aligned with the faction and switches sides. The player experiences the villains ideology from the inside before rejecting it. Reveals hidden lore and the full picture of the story.

**Note:** The Defector role is reserved for post-launch or late development. The other three roles must be fully written first as the Defector arc depends on their context.

---

## Unlock System
- Each role has a pool of 5 starters
- Default starter is available immediately
- Beating a run unlocks a random starter from that roles pool
- Legendary starters have a 10% unlock chance
- If only the legendary starter remains locked, next unlock is guaranteed (100%)
- Beating a role 4 times guarantees the legendary unlock path
