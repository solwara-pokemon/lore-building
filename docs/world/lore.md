# World Lore

## Canon Basis
Solwara is set in a world where the events of **Pokémon Sapphire were not stopped**. Archie succeeded in awakening Kyogre, the rain never ceased, and sea levels rose catastrophically over the following generations.

## The Flood
The flooding was not instantaneous — it happened over decades. The grandparents of todays inhabitants remember dry land. There are photographs, oral histories, and ruins of the old world still visible beneath the waves.

The region most affected is **Oceania** — Australia, New Zealand, Papua New Guinea, Indonesia — now a vast ocean with scattered land patches. The largest usable land masses are approximately 250 miles across, representing the peaks of mountain ranges, volcanic highlands, and elevated terrain that survived the inundation.

## Kyogre
Kyogre is not a villain. The world has a complicated relationship with it:
- **The Unforgotten Party** — correctly identifies Kyogre as a victim, weaponized by Team Aqua and hated unfairly by Team Magma. Pokémon are not at fault.
- **General population** — mixed feelings, some instinctive blame
- **The truth** — Kyogre is the only living witness to what actually happened to Groudon

## Groudon
Groudon is dead. It died during the conflict with Kyogre — the event the world calls "The War." Nobody knows this. The Unforgotten Party searches for Groudon believing restoration is possible. Their hope is built on a tragedy no one has discovered yet.

Whether the truth was covered up or simply lost is unknown — but the answer is that **nobody knows**. Everyone is acting in good faith toward an impossible goal.

## The World Now
Two to three generations have passed since The War. A new culture has formed:
- Underwater cities built into drowned ruins
- Surface settlements on land patches
- A maritime economy connecting the scattered islands
- Deep political division between those who have accepted the new world and those who want the old one back
